Exports include: farmer name, daily quantities (Day 1–31), total qty, unit price, total pay, account number, and annual bonus calculation.
Excel Column Structure
A: Farmer Name
B: Farmer ID
C: Crop Type
D: Account Number
E–AI: Day 1 – Day 31 quantities
AJ: Total Quantity =SUM(E:AI)
AK: Unit Price (KSh)
AL: Total Pay =AJ*AK
AM: Yearly Qty (cumulative)
AN: Bonus =IF(AM>2000, AL_yearly*0.05, 0)
